About Me

I'm Shruthi Arogyaswamy — an educator and college student who genuinely loves working with children and believes the best learning happens through play, stories, and exploration. My motivation

Why Novo Camps?

Applying for

Sr Leader in Training (Paid Internship)

Shadow facilitators · Co-lead activities · Manage small groups · Learn real camp operations across Acting, Making & Spirit tracks

When I read about Novo Camps' i-Can philosophy, I felt it immediately — because it's the same belief I carry into every interaction with a child. That confidence grows through participation, not performance. That the process matters more than the product. That every single child has something incredible waiting to come out.

My time at Footprints introduced me to structured, research-backed learning with very young children. Novo Camps excites me because it extends that same intentionality to ages 6–14 — the exact years when identity starts forming, when self-belief either takes root or gets shaken.

I'm drawn to all three tracks. In Theatre & Story Lab, I see children discovering their voice. In the Makers Studio, I see them learning that failure is just the first draft of success. In Outdoor Fun, I see teamwork and joy in its purest form.

The Sr Leaders in Training role is exactly where I want to be — learning from the Imagineerz team, shadowing experienced facilitators, and doing the real, hands-on work of building a camp day that children will remember. I'm a fast learner, a team player, and someone who shows up with full energy and full heart.
